Audience

The target audience is anyone wanting more information about the community, historical or current day. Historical information, popular attractions, business, services, churches and community resources.

Personas

  • Community Members wanting information to help them in their community.
  • All tourist: passing through, or those planning their stay.

Scenarios

  • Someone looking for information about how and when the community was founded could find useful information in our historical content.
  • Tourist wondering what their is to do and see.
  • Members of the community wanting to find businesses and services to fill their needs.
  • Tourist wanting to find businesss and services that can help them with their stay in our community.
  • Community members or tourist wanting information about churches, public resources and community resources available to the public.
